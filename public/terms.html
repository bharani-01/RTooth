2. Clinical Platform Scope and Limitations

RTooth provides software designed to organize case board tracking, patient compliance tracking, biopsy schedule logs, and digital health history records. However, RTooth does not practice medicine and does not provide clinical services.

All clinical evaluations, diagnosis updates, biopsy indicators, surgical decisions, and treatment regimens are the sole professional responsibility of the attending oncologist and licensed dental surgeon. The Platform is an aid to care coordination and does not replace medical judgment.

3. Practitioner Verification and Licensing

To use RTooth as a medical specialist, you must verify your identity and professional medical licensing. You agree to:

  • Provide accurate, current registration details including medical license identifiers.
  • Keep your login credentials strictly confidential to prevent unauthorized access to Patient Health Information (PHI).
  • Notify RTooth administration immediately at admin@rtooth.com if you suspect unauthorized account access or a HIPAA security boundary breach.

4. Patient Self-Assessment Disclaimer

Patient tools, including the interactive Self-Assessment screening wizard, are for educational screening purposes only. The risk calculations do not constitute a diagnosis or surgical recommendation. Patients are instructed to consult a qualified oncologist or dental surgeon for comprehensive diagnostic evaluation.

5. Terminations and Access Controls

RTooth reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate HIPAA regulations, engage in unauthorized data queries, fail to verify professional medical credentials, or attempt to bypass database Row Level Security (RLS) layers.
